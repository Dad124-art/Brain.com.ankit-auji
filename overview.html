Brain Overview

The brain is the most complex organ in the human body. It controls every aspect of our behavior, thoughts, and actions. Understanding the brain helps us learn how it drives both simple and intricate processes.

Key Functions:

  • Processing sensory information
  • Regulating emotions and mood
  • Controlling voluntary and involuntary movements
  • Storing and recalling memories
  • Making decisions and solving problems

Brain Structure Overview:

The brain is divided into several important areas, each responsible for different functions:

  • Cerebrum: The largest part, responsible for thoughts, memory, reasoning, and movement.
  • Cerebellum: Controls coordination, balance, and fine motor skills.
  • Brainstem: Regulates basic functions such as heartbeat, breathing, and digestion.
