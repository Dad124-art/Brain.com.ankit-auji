Neurologist

A neurologist is a medical doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Roles of a Neurologist:

  • Diagnosis: Diagnoses neurological disorders like epilepsy, Alzheimer's, and Parkinson's disease.
  • Treatment: Provides treatments including medication, therapy, and sometimes surgery.
  • Prevention: Offers advice on preventing neurological conditions and maintaining brain health.
  • Research: Conducts research to discover new treatments and understand brain functions.
