Brain Anatomy

The brain is an incredibly complex organ, and its structure is essential for its functions. Here are the major parts of the brain:

Main Parts of the Brain:

  • Cerebrum: The largest part, responsible for reasoning, motor skills, and higher cognitive functions.
  • Cerebellum: Controls balance, coordination, and fine motor skills.
  • Brainstem: Regulates basic functions such as breathing, heartbeat, and sleep patterns.
  • Thalamus: Acts as a relay station for sensory information.
  • Hypothalamus: Regulates body temperature, hunger, thirst, and hormone release.
