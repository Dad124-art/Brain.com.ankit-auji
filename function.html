Brain Functions

The brain controls many vital functions in the human body, from sensory processing to regulating emotions and movements. Below are the key functions of the brain:

Key Functions of the Brain:

  • Cognition: Thought, reasoning, memory, and problem-solving.
  • Movement: Control over voluntary and involuntary movements.
  • Sensory Processing: Processing information from the five senses.
  • Emotion: Regulation of emotions such as happiness, anger, and fear.
  • Homeostasis: Maintaining balance, like body temperature and sleep cycles.
